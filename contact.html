Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I schedule an installation?

expand_more
After placing your order, our team will contact you within 24 hours to schedule a convenient installation time. You can also reach out via WhatsApp at +91 98215 60609 for immediate scheduling.

What areas do you provide service in?

expand_more
We provide installation and service across all major cities in India. For specific area coverage, please contact our customer support team with your location details.

How long does delivery take?

expand_more
Delivery typically takes 3-7 business days depending on your location. Metro cities receive faster delivery (2-4 days). You'll receive tracking details once your order is dispatched.

Do you offer bulk discounts for apartments/societies?

expand_more
Yes! We offer special pricing for bulk orders and housing societies. Please contact our wholesale team via the wholesale page or email us at info@fingaurd.com for custom quotations.

What payment methods do you accept?

expand_more
We accept Cash on Delivery, UPI, Bank Transfer, Credit/Debit Cards, and Net Banking. Payment must be made in full before delivery.
