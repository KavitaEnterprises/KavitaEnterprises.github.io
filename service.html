Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our service and AMC plans

How often should I service my RO water purifier? expand_more

We recommend servicing your RO system every 3 months for optimal performance. This includes filter cleaning, TDS testing, and general maintenance checks.

When should I replace the RO membrane? expand_more

RO membranes typically last 18-24 months depending on water quality and usage. Our technicians will test and recommend replacement during service visits.

What is covered under AMC plans? expand_more

AMC plans cover regular service visits, filter cleaning, TDS testing, and discounts on spare parts. Premium plans also include free filters and emergency service.

Do you provide service for other brands? expand_more

Yes, our expert technicians can service most RO brands. However, we only guarantee genuine parts and comprehensive support for FinGaurd products.

What is your service response time? expand_more

Our standard response time is 48 hours on business days only. Service is available 11 AM - 5 PM (Monday to Saturday). We're closed on Sundays and public holidays. AMC and service are available only in Delhi region.
