Our Story

Building a healthier India, one family at a time

Founded in 2008, FinGaurd was born from a simple mission: to provide every Indian household with access to pure, safe drinking water. Understanding the unique challenges of Indian water quality, we set out to design RO systems specifically engineered for local conditions.

What started as a small manufacturing unit in Delhi has grown into a nationwide network serving over 50,000 families. Our commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction has made us one of India's most trusted names in water purification.

Today, FinGaurd continues to push the boundaries of water purification technology, ensuring that every drop of water you drink is pure, safe, and healthy for your family.
